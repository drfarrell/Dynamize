Dynamize

Branding guidelines for the new University of Denver startup incubator

Abstract

Dynamize is a student-run startup incubator at the University of Denver that provides resources, guidance, and capital to aspiring student entrepreneurs. We wanted to make entrepreneurship fun, exciting, and appealing to students across all majors, so we created a distinct brand for use everywhere Dynamize is represented.


The logo is an attempt to create an inclusive community across all majors
